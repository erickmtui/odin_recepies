CHIPSI

Description


This is a local fine recepie of potatoes that is commonly used in Tanzania. It is a local favourite among the Tanzanians. Here is its recepie.

INGRIDIENTS

  • potatoes
  • salt
  • cooking oil
  • eggs

STEPS TO MAKE

  1. prepare the potatoes
  2. slice the potatoes as if making french fries then fry them
  3. break and mix the eggs in a separate bowl
  4. on top of the drenched fries add the eggs
  5. let it sit for some time then turn upside down
  6. serve while hot
